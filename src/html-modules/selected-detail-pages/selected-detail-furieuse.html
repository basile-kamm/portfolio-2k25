Furieuse is a french book telling the story of Isabelle, a princess which flees the castle, marriage and her dad, in quest of finding happiness and her lost sister...

This project's goal was to create an innovativing storytelling, which could be transposed both in a website and in a video. To do so, we decided to focus on the caracters of the book, and show how they're connected.
