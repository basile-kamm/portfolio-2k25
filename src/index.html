About me and my life

Born on May 19, 2006, I grew up surrounded by the untamed beauty of the Gruyère region, where nature and cows were part of my daily life. Early on, I developed a passion for video games and digital media, which led me to pursue studies at eikon in Fribourg. Once I began, I quickly developed a strong interest in web development—a passion that still continues to grow.
Now in my third year of apprenticeship as an Interactive Media Designer, I’m focused on finding an intership to specialize in graphic design, web design, and especially in frontend and backend development.
