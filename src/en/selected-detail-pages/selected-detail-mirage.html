Mirage Festival

Graphic design

UI/UX design

Mirage Festival is an annual event in Lyon (France) which explores numeric arts, electronic culture and new technologies. As one of France leading festivals for digital creation, the Mirage Festival is looking to modernize its visual identity. The aim of the redesign is to create a more contemporary, dynamic and impactful image that will appeal to a varied audience while keeping the avant-garde spirit of the festival.

This work gave us great freedom and a wide range of possibilities. We chose to create a highly unstructured, high-contrast visual using image-distortion techniques such as datamoshing using supermosh.io. The aim was to question the limits of design, image and IA, by creating a website mock-up, an animated poster and some stickerz.
