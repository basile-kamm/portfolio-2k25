Tatitotu is an educative website, allowing teachers and students to easily access lists of verbs. As a teacher, you can create templates and print exercices sheets. As a student, you can directly train on the website and play games to improve your french verb knowledges.

This work gave me great fun to code, as I focused on having a simple and intuitive design. I then could play with JavaScript and a color theme switcher, giving more life and personalisation of the website. I only focused on the frontend part, so sadly none of the backend is working.
