Snow & Moutains

Nitecore is an advertinssing spot showcasing the headlight Nitecore. We decided to show the adventure that the headlight could enable us. To do so, we went filming in the moutains in the middle of the winter...

It was a super fun to work on, because we really went out of our comfort zone by filming directly in the mountains with really harsh conditions. This challenging experience makes us even prouder of our ad.
